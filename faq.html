Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our clinic and appointments.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book online using our booking page or contact our reception team directly.

Do you offer same-day appointments?

We offer a limited number of same-day appointments depending on availability.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring identification, relevant medical information and details of any current medication.

Can I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

Yes, please contact the clinic as early as possible if you need to change your booking.

Where is the clinic located?

We are located in Cathedral Quarter, Belfast, with good access to local transport links.

Do you provide online consultations?

Some appointments may be available online depending on the service required.
